About

A little context on who I am and the kind of work I enjoy building.

I’m a Computer Science graduate and developer focused on game development, tooling, and technical problem solving.

I enjoy building systems that improve workflow as much as I enjoy building player-facing features. That includes gameplay prototypes, editor tools, validation systems, and technical write-ups that explain the thinking behind the work.

This site exists to show both the outcome and the process: the project itself, the design tradeoffs, and what I learned while making it.
